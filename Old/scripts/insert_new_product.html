STORED PROCEDURE EXAMPLE

This form inserts a row into the PRODUCT table, - then displays a list of products .


The form accepts the input values, posts them to a session variable $_POST['productid'], $_POST['description'] then launches stored_procedure_1.php

Next, stored_procedure_1.php reads $_POST['productid'], $_POST['description'] into the PHP variables $productid, $description and later binds them to the Oracle bind variables :prodid and :descrip

The PL/SQL stored procedure add_product() is called using :prodid and :descrip as its parameters. This performs the insert.



After the insert, the PL/SQL stored procedure list_products() is called using :prodlistbv (datatype sys_refcursor) as its parameter.
This displays the full product list.
